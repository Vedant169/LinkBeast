Our Story

We started by helping
local Indian businesses grow.

From a small digital services company in India to building the country's first unified marketing ecosystem. This is the story of ConnectSutra Digital and how LinkBeast was born.

Our Mission

Democratise digital marketing for every Indian SME.

Tools built for global markets don't work for local Indian businesses. We're building the infrastructure that lets an SME in Jaipur compete with brands in Mumbai — with verified creators, transparent pricing, and real data.

The Story

From local agency to national platform.

ConnectSutra Digital was founded with one goal: to help Indian small businesses grow digitally without paying premium agency prices or getting lost in complex tools built for global markets.

After working with 20+ paying clients across Delhi, Jaipur, and Bengaluru, we saw the same patterns — brands wasted hours finding influencers via DMs, hired unvetted freelancers who disappeared, and had no way to see what was working across their marketing stack.

We didn't build another tool. We built an ecosystem — one that connects brands, creators, and service providers in a single, accountable platform. That's LinkBeast.

Our Journey
2023

ConnectSutra Digital Founded

Started as a lean digital marketing services company focused on helping Indian SMEs with SEO, social media, and content creation.

2024 — Q1

First 10 Paying Clients

Expanded to 10 paying clients across Delhi and Jaipur. Started identifying the core pain points around vendor fragmentation and creator accountability.

2024 — Q3

The LinkBeast Concept Born

After client after client asked "can you handle influencers too?", we realised the market needed a unified platform, not just another service.

2025

Launched the platform with the 5-pillar ecosystem, creator tier system, and brand marketplace. 20+ brands onboarded at launch.

LinkBeast Studios

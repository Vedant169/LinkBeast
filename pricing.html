Good to Know

Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

Is GST included in the prices?

All listed prices are exclusive of GST. 18% GST will be applicable as per Indian tax regulations.

Are there lock-in contracts?

Starter plans have no lock-in. Growth and Scale bundles require a 3-month minimum for best results. We recommend a 6-month commitment for SEO.

What payment methods are accepted?

UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), and credit/debit cards in ₹ INR. We do not accept international payment methods.

Can I customise a package?

Absolutely. Contact our team and we'll build a custom plan for your brand's specific needs and budget.
